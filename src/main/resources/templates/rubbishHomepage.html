Sorting Rubbish

  • Green bin

    Used to dispose of organic waste. However, they should not be used for plastic bags, gardening tools or flower pots.

  • Yellow bin

    Used for recyclable waste, fed into a high-tech machine that analyses the waste and separates it into different types.

  • Red bin

    Designed to hold general household waste. This type of waste takes a long time to decompose and is the most harmful to the environment, so make sure you protect the environment by putting your waste in biodegradable bags.
